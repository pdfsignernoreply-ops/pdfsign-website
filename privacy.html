Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective from: January 2026

🔒 Key promise: Your PDF documents are signed entirely on your local machine. No document, file, or its contents are ever uploaded to our servers. Your private key never leaves your DSC token.

This Privacy Policy describes how Tender Training India (GSTIN: 27ABZPW8421B1Z3), operating as PDFSign.in, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our software or visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information only when necessary to provide our service:

  • Name and email address — collected at checkout to deliver your licence key and send transactional emails (invoice, renewal reminders).
  • Phone number — optional, collected only if you provide it for support purposes.
  • GSTIN (GST Identification Number) — optional, collected at checkout if you wish to receive a GST invoice for tax set-off purposes.
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Razorpay. We never see or store your card details, UPI ID, or banking credentials.
  • Machine fingerprint (hardware ID) — a one-way hash derived from your device's hardware properties. This is used solely to enforce licence seat limits and detect duplicate activations. It cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Licence activation records — which machine(s) activated a licence and when, used for team licence seat management.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your PDF documents or their contents — signing happens 100% locally on your device.
  • Your DSC private key — it never leaves your physical token.
  • Browsing history or behaviour beyond what Google Analytics records (see Section 6).
  • Any biometric or sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Licence delivery — sending your licence key by email after purchase.
  • GST invoice — generating and emailing a GST-compliant tax invoice (SAC 997331) if you provided your GSTIN at checkout. Invoices are issued under Tender Training India, GSTIN 27ABZPW8421B1Z3.
  • Renewal reminders — we may email you approximately 7 days before your licence expires to give you time to renew without interruption. You can request to opt out at any time.
  • Product updates — notifying you of important software updates or security patches.
  • Support — responding to your queries via email or WhatsApp.
  • Fraud prevention — detecting licence misuse or duplicate activations.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is listed above. We do not sell or share your personal data with any third party.

4. Reseller Portal

PDFSign.in operates a reseller programme for authorised DSC service providers. If you purchase through a reseller:

  • Your name and email are collected by the reseller to generate your licence. Your email is used to send you the licence key directly from PDFSign.in.
  • The reseller does not receive your payment details; Razorpay handles all transactions.
  • The wholesale price paid by the reseller is not disclosed to you in any communication from PDFSign.in.

5. Coupon Codes

If you use a coupon or discount code at checkout, we record the code used alongside your licence record for accounting and fraud-prevention purposes. No additional personal data is collected in this process.

6. Analytics & Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 on our website to understand aggregate visitor behaviour (pages visited, traffic sources, country). Google Analytics uses cookies and may collect your IP address. This data is anonymised and used only for improving our website.

We use no other tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics tools. Session data within the admin portal is stored in your browser's sessionStorage and is never sent to third-party services.

7. Data Storage & Security

  • Licence and account data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted in Singapore (ap-southeast-1 region), with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Payment records are maintained by Razorpay in accordance with RBI guidelines and PCI-DSS standards.
  • Emails are sent via Google Workspace (Gmail SMTP). Email content is not retained beyond delivery.
  • We retain your data for as long as your licence is active and for up to 3 years thereafter for legal and accounting compliance under the Companies Act and GST laws.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Opt out of renewal reminder emails at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at help@pdfsign.in. We will respond within 7 business days.

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Razorpay — payment processing (razorpay.com/privacy)
  • Supabase — database hosting (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Google Analytics — website analytics (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Vercel — API hosting (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Cloudflare — website hosting and DDoS protection (cloudflare.com/privacypolicy)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our software or website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
