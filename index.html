FAQ

Common questions

Q Which Windows versions are supported?

PDFSign.in runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit). It requires .NET 10 Runtime, which the installer will download automatically if not present.

Q Are my PDFs uploaded to your servers?

No. Signing happens 100% on your machine. Your documents never leave your computer. We don't have access to your files or your DSC token's private key.

Q Will my signed PDFs be accepted on government portals?

Yes. PDFSign.in uses PAdES (PDF Advanced Electronic Signatures) which is accepted on GeM, CPPP, e-Procurement, MCA21, Income Tax, and all major Indian government portals.

Q Which DSC token brands are supported?

PDFSign.in works with all major USB DSC tokens used in India — HyperPKI (HYP2003), ePass 2003/3003, Proxkey, Feitian, WD Pro, Watchdata, and any PKCS#11-compatible token issued by a licensed CA under CCA India.

Q Can I sign on behalf of a company (organization signature)?

Yes. If your DSC token has an organization certificate, PDFSign.in will show it as a signing option. The signed PDF will show your name and organization in the signature block.
