Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Our commitment

We want you to be completely satisfied with PDFSign.in. If you are not happy for any reason, we will refund your purchase — no questions asked.

Eligibility

All plans — Monthly (₹499), Annual (₹2,999), and Lifetime (₹7,999) — are eligible for a full refund within 7 days of the original purchase date.

How to request a refund

Email us at help@pdfsign.in with the subject line "Refund Request" and include your order ID (from your Razorpay receipt). We will process your refund within 24 hours of receiving your request.

Refund timeline

Once approved, refunds are credited back to your original payment method within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

After a refund

Once a refund is processed, your licence key will be deactivated. You may re-purchase at any time.

Exceptions

Refund requests made after 7 days of purchase will be considered on a case-by-case basis. We do not offer partial refunds for unused days on Monthly or Annual plans.
